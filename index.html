Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyAgriBot free to use?

Yes, MyAgriBot is completely free to use. All you need is the Telegram app installed on your device.

What crops are supported?

MyAgriBot supports a wide range of crops commonly grown in India, including rice, wheat, cotton, sugarcane, pulses, and various vegetables and fruits.

How accurate is the weather information?

The weather information is sourced from WeatherAPI.com, which provides highly accurate forecasts. However, as with all weather forecasts, there may be slight variations.

Does the bot work offline?

No, you need an internet connection to interact with the bot as it fetches real-time data from various sources.
